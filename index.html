基础FFT应用题

Description

给出$n$个数$q_1, q_2, \cdots, q_n$,定义：

$$F_j = \sum_{i=1}^{j-1} \frac {q_i \times q_j}{(i - j)^2} - \sum_{i=j+1}^n \frac {q_i \times q_j}{(i-j)^2}$$

$$E_i = \frac {F_i}{q_i}$$

求$E_1, E_2, \cdots E_n$, $n \leq 10^5, q_i \leq 10^9$.
